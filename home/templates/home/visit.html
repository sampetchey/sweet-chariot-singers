Rehearsals and what to expect...

Location and Time

Rehearsals take place Monday Evenings at 7.30pm in the Practice Hall at Kneller Hall, Twickenham, TW2 7DU

Choir Parts

Sweet Chariot Singers is an all female choir. We sing in many styles and in various part arrangements

Singers are divied into:

Tenor - often a descant harmony over the melody

Lead - the lead part, often singing the principle melody

Baritone - in a similar range to the lead, but often in harmony with it. Written in the bass clef

Bass - the lowest part providing the root harmony
